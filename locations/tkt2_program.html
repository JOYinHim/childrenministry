Program

TK Trans 2

Program TK Trans 2 dirancang untuk membantu anak-anak berkembang secara holistik dalam iman, kreativitas, dan kecerdasan sosial. The TK Trans 2 program is designed to support children's holistic development in faith, creativity, and social intelligence. TK Trans 2 프로그램은 아이들의 신앙, 창의성, 사회적 지능의 전인적 발달을 지원하도록 설계되었습니다.
